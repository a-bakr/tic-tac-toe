# Sound Effects for Tic Tac Toe Game

You need to add the following sound files to this directory:

1. player-move.mp3 - Sound when the player makes a move
2. ai-move.mp3 - Sound when the AI makes a move
3. win.mp3 - Sound when the player wins
4. lose.mp3 - Sound when the AI wins
5. draw.mp3 - Sound when the game ends in a draw

You can find free sound effects at:
- https://freesound.org/
- https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/
- https://www.zapsplat.com/

Recommended sounds:
- Player move: Short click or pop sound
- AI move: Slightly different click or pop sound
- Win: Short victory jingle
- Lose: Short defeat sound
- Draw: Neutral notification sound

After adding the sound files, make sure they match the filenames referenced in the JavaScript code.
